# Midjourney Portrait Prompts -- Story 3 Path B & C Enemies

Enemy portraits for the two new Story 3 branching paths. 28 total portraits needed.

## Style Reference

The enemy portrait style is darker and more painterly than the player class style.

**Style suffix to append to every prompt:**
```
dark fantasy RPG character portrait, full body, dark background with subtle teal glow, painterly digital art style, stylized proportions, moody dramatic lighting, game asset --ar 3:4 --s 750
```

**Key style notes from existing enemy portraits:**
- Dark backgrounds with soft teal/cyan backlighting and rim lighting
- Painterly digital rendering, slightly stylized (not photorealistic)
- Full body shots, characters centered on subtle ground plane
- Muted palette: teal, dark blue, earth tones with selective warm accents (red sashes, gold trim, amber glows)
- Atmospheric fog at ground level
- More texture and grit than the clean player class portraits

Save images as `{filename}` into `EchoesOfChoice/assets/art/portraits/enemies/`
